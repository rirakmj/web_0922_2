2. Bichon Frise


  • Bichon Frise is a dog breed from Catalonia, Spain.
  • Its name comes from the French words “Bichon” which means “beech” and “Frise” which means “curl”.
  • The Bichon Frise has a tricolor coat.
  • Its body is long and slender.
  • Its legs are short and compact.
  • The Bichon Frise is a cheerful, intelligent, and obedient dog.
