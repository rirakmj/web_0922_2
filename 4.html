4. Pekingese

  • Pekingese is a dog breed from China.
  • Pekingese has a flat face, big eyes, and curly tails.
  • Its body is covered with a dense, soft, and glossy coat.
  • Its eyes are black.
  • The Pekingese are affectionate and gentle.
